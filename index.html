Key rules

What actually moves the needle

1

Hit protein every single day

140–150g minimum. This protects muscle and drives growth regardless of which phase you're in. Calories can flex — protein cannot.

2

Progressive overload every session

Add 1–2 reps or a small weight increase every week. When you hit the top of the rep range, add weight and drop back down. Log everything.

3

Stay in the right calorie range

2,200 kcal during recomp. Once in the lean bulk, eat closer to 2,800. Adjust every 4 weeks based on how your body is responding.

4

Check in every 4 weeks

Same day, same time. Weight + waist measurement + optional photo. Trends over weeks matter — not daily fluctuations.
