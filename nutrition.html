Key rules

What to remember

1

Protein first, always

Hit 140–150g protein before anything else. Once protein is covered, fill remaining calories however you like — including sweet treats.

2

Training vs rest days

On gym days eat 2,200 kcal and keep carbs around your workout. On rest days, drop the pre-workout banana and reduce rice slightly — around 1,950–2,000 kcal.

3

5,000 steps minimum

At 5,000 steps per day you're in the lightly active bracket — this is what your 2,200 kcal target is calibrated to. Push to 8–10k on rest days for accelerated fat loss.

4

Add creatine daily

5g creatine monohydrate stirred into your overnight oats or protein shake every day. Tasteless, cheap, and one of the only supplements with solid evidence behind it.
