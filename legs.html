Wednesday · ~60 minutes

Legs Day

Quads · Hamstrings · Glutes · Calves

The biggest session of the week. Lead with hack squat or leg press while your legs are completely fresh, then hammer the hamstrings from two angles. Don't skip calves.

1

Hack squat or leg press

quads glutes
4 × 8–10 Rest 2–3 min

Feet low and narrow on the platform for maximum quad focus. Control the descent — don't let the weight drop. Full depth if your mobility allows.

2

Lying leg curl

hamstrings
4 × 10–12 Rest 90 sec

Curl heels to glutes, squeeze at the top, then lower slowly over 3 seconds. The eccentric (lowering phase) is where most hamstring growth happens — don't rush it.

Leg extension

quads
3 × 12–15 Rest 60 sec

Pause and squeeze at the top for 1 second on every rep. Pure quad isolation — you should feel a strong burn by rep 12. Control the weight on the way down.

Walking lunges

3 × 10/leg Rest 90 sec

Keep torso upright throughout. Knee tracks over your second toe — don't let it cave inward. Full stride length activates more glute. Use dumbbells to add load.
